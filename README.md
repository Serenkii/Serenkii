Hi, I'm Marian. I'm a physics graduate from the University of Konstanz who enjoys programming both as a tool and as a hobby.

This profile is a mix of personal projects and academic work. On the hobby side you'll find things like a JavaFX tower defence game and some Arduino experiments. On the more research-oriented side, there's a beam profile analyser I wrote during a laser physics internship, built around the DataRay API.

My bachelor's thesis was in computational condensed matter physics, simulating altermagnets with relativistic corrections. Our results pointed to a Spin Nernst effect in altermagnets.
